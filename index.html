Jesus Christ

The way, the truth, and the life.

Timeline and achievments of Jesus Christ

  • 4–6 B.C.– Born in Bethlehem.
  • 26–27 A.D.– Baptized by John the Baptist and began His ministry.
  • 27–30 A.D.– Taught about love, forgiveness, and the Kingdom of God.
  • 30 A.D.– Shared the Last Supper with His disciples.
  • 30 A.D.– Crucified in Jerusalem.
  • 30 A.D.– Rose from the dead on the third day.
  • 40 Days Later– Ascended into heaven.
